Skidoo Boardgame

Skidoo is my vibrant graphic design endeavor, a playground where creativity thrives. Colour reigns supreme, but balance is key. I tempered saturation for a gentle allure. Rounded edges offer a welcoming feel, inviting children to explore. The logo, a beacon of simplicity, embraces negative and positive space, embodying Skidoo's essence with clean lines. The Characters features beloved penguins that harmonize with the brand's palette. Skidoo isn't just a project; it's a testament to my passion for graphic design. With meticulous attention to detail, I've crafted a world where imagination flourishes, inviting young adventurers on unforgettable journeys.
