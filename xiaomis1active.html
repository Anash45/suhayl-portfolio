Xiaomi S1 Active

This personal design project is based on the concept of a fusion of sports and a playful atmosphere, aiming to make sports feel like a fun, engaging experience while simultaneously promoting the Xiaomi smartwatch. My approach was to seamlessly blend this vibrant energy with Xiaomi's brand identity capturing its flowing, zen-like minimalism, while ensuring the visuals are eye-catching and memorable. The result is a design that not only resonates with the target audience but also stays true to Xiaomi's signature aesthetic.
