LVCD map project

The Director of LVDC encountered a pressing issue regarding visitor navigation within the park. It became evident that the current guides weren't sufficient, resulting in frequent disorientation among visitors. Determined to provide a solution, I embarked on a mission to enhance their journey. Upon examining the existing park map, it became apparent that it lacked clarity and usability. Visitors struggled to navigate effectively, often resorting to asking for directions. To address this, I conceived the idea of creating a specialized walking map, featuring clearly marked locations from A to J. This map would serve as a user-friendly guide, ensuring visitors could explore the park with ease.

To refine this concept, surveys were conducted with visitors in the park to gather insights on their navigation needs. Many expressed a desire for additional signage throughout the park. Taking this feedback into account, I set out to design a new map, complete with pin locations and enhanced signage. The result was a comprehensive navigation system that transformed the visitor experience. With the new map in hand and improved signage throughout the park, visitors could now navigate confidently, free from the frustration of getting lost. It was a gratifying achievement, demonstrating the power of design to enhance user satisfaction and improve overall park enjoyment.
